🎵 Welcome to We Wear Our Pride!

This song hides real Australian sporting teams inside clever, poetic lyrics. One single word — like "marsupials" — can secretly stand for ten different national teams!
For example, including all the ones with "-roos" in their name!
No team or sport is named directly*. It's all hidden in plain sight, waiting to be decoded.

* with two notable exceptions — how quickly can you find them? 🙂

How to play:

  • 👉 Pick a team or sport from the lists
  • 📖 Press & hold any name to learn more about it
  • 📖 Double-click any name to learn more about it
  • 🔍 Find where it's hiding in the lyrics and click there to match
  • 🟢 Green words are clues — complete a set to celebrate the Green & Gold!
  • 🥇 Win medals as you go — see how many you can collect!
